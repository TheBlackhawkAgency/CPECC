About the Council

The China Pakistan Economic Corridor Council (CPECC) is a registered society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan. It operates as a non-profit, non-political, and non-commercial organization dedicated to promoting structured cooperation between Pakistan and China.

The Council focuses on socio-economic development through education, research, healthcare, and public welfare initiatives while facilitating institutional collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Our Identity

The CPECC identity represents unity, strategic partnership, and shared development goals between Pakistan and China, reflecting a commitment to long-term collaboration and sustainable growth.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading platform fostering sustainable development, knowledge exchange, and strategic collaboration between Pakistan and China.

Mission

To advance education, research, social welfare, and community development through partnerships and inclusive programs.

Strategic Objectives

Bilateral Cooperation

Promote cultural, academic, and economic collaboration between Pakistan and China while facilitating partnerships among institutions, professionals, and communities.

Education & Knowledge Development

Establish and support schools, universities, and research centers, while organizing seminars, conferences, and publishing educational materials.

Healthcare & Social Welfare

Develop hospitals, clinics, and community health centers, while supporting vulnerable groups and providing scholarships and financial assistance.

Community Infrastructure

Establish libraries, laboratories, and public learning spaces while promoting literacy and awareness programs at the community level.

Humanitarian & Philanthropic Work

Implement poverty alleviation initiatives, rehabilitation programs, shelters, and social protection frameworks for underserved communities.

Programs and Initiatives

The Council undertakes academic and research collaborations, organizes workshops, seminars, and conferences, and implements healthcare and welfare projects. It also focuses on publication of knowledge resources and capacity-building initiatives to support long-term development.

Governance Structure & Financial Model

Governance Framework

CPECC is managed by a governing body and executive committee comprising experienced professionals and stakeholders aligned with the Council’s mission and objectives.

Transparency & Accountability

The organization operates under a transparent decision-making process with a strong emphasis on accountability, ethical practices, and institutional integrity.

Sources of Funding

Funding is secured through donations, grants, membership contributions, and partnerships with local and international organizations.

Utilization of Funds

All financial resources are fully allocated toward achieving organizational objectives, with no profit distribution and adherence to governing regulations.
